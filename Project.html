SMART WATER QUALITY MONITORING SYSTEM

This innovative system integrates Internet of Things (IoT) technology to collect, transmit, and analyze real-time data related to water quality. Through a network of sensors strategically placed in various water sources, the system continuously measures parameters such as pH levels, turbidity, temperature, and the presence of contaminants. These data are transmitted to a central server via the IoT infrastructure.
